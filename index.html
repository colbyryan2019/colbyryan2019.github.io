Hi, I'm Colby Ryan — a Software Engineer at Panoramix Financial living in New York City, NY. I graduated from Union College in 2024 with a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics.

At Panoramix, I led development of FacetAI — an AI-powered assistant announced at the T3 conference — and have built a range of full-stack features including a Retrieval Augmented Generation bot, nearest-neighbor search systems for embedded text retrieval, and user-facing investment model tooling.

Before that, I interned at PanAgora Asset Management as a data science intern and at TandemAI working on HPC storage performance. After high school, I spent a gap year in Barcelona, Spain, where I earned my TEFL certification and taught English.

Outside of work, I enjoy building mobile apps, traveling, playing soccer, basketball, and chess.
