ABOUT COLBY RYAN

My name is Colby Ryan and I am from Southborough, Massachusetts. I am currently a junior at Union College with a double major in Mathematics and Computer Science. I took my first computer science class in middle school and quickly discovered my passion for computer programming. In high school, I took AP Computer Science as a sophomore, and then proceeded to take several post-AP computer science courses. After graduating high school in 2019, I decided to take a gap year in Barcelona where I taught English, but it was cut short due to the coronavirus pandemic. I started at Union College in the fall of 2020, with the intention of majoring in Computer Science. After taking my first theoretical mathematics classes, I realized I enjoy the logic behind theoretical math, and declared a double major in both Mathematics and Computer Science, and I am now in my junior year. I have been involved on campus through joining a fraternity, sitting on multiple committee’s, and being elected class Vice President. Some of the classes I have taken include Data Structures, Algorithms, Text Analytics, Topics in Discrete Math, Group Theory, and Set Theory and Logic.
